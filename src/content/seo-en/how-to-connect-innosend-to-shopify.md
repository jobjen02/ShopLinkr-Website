<h2>Connecting Innosend to Shopify: how it works</h2>
<p>The connection runs through your shipping tool's settings. At a high level, you do this:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log in to your account and open the integrations or connections section.</li>
<li>Find the Shopify integration and choose "connect".</li>
<li>Authorize the app to link your Shopify store.</li>
<li>Set which orders get imported and which carrier you create labels with.</li>
</ol>
<p>After that, your Shopify orders flow in and you create shipping labels for them. The track and trace you generate is sent back to Shopify by your shipping tool, so your customer automatically receives a shipping confirmation.</p>
