<h2>What the connection delivers</h2>
<p>bol is a large Dutch sales channel, but managing bol on its own alongside your other channels takes time and invites mistakes. Connecting bol to ShopLinkr brings everything together:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Central order processing.</strong> All your <a href="/en/features/orders">bol orders</a> land in one overview, next to orders from Shopify, WooCommerce, Kaufland, and other channels.</li>
<li><strong>Real-time inventory.</strong> Sell a product on bol and the <a href="/en/features/inventory">stock</a> drops instantly across every channel, so you never oversell. Stock forecasting, fictional stock, and unlimited stock are part of it too.</li>
<li><strong>Labels in one click.</strong> Print shipping labels for your <a href="/en/features/carriers">carrier</a> (PostNL, DPD, MyParcel, Sendcloud, and more). AutoPrint generates them automatically, and track & trace returns to bol on its own.</li>
<li><strong>Faster picking and packing.</strong> Work with <a href="/en/features/pick-lists">pick lists</a> that follow a smart route through your warehouse, with barcode scanning, bins, and packing stations.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to connect Sendy to bol</h2>
<p>The connection runs through bol's API, so you'll need the credentials from your bol seller account. From there it goes like this:</p>
<ol>
<li>Create a ShopLinkr account or log in.</li>
<li>Open your sales channels and add bol as a new channel.</li>
<li>Enter your bol API details to authorize the connection.</li>
<li>Link your carrier(s) so you can create labels for bol orders right away.</li>
<li>Save. Your orders, products, and stock now sync automatically.</li>
</ol>
<p>From that point on, you process every bol order inside ShopLinkr. Want to add more channels or carriers? Just repeat the same steps.</p>
