<h2>What are Sendy and WooCommerce?</h2>
<p>Sendy is shipping software that helps businesses send their parcels efficiently. It connects with carriers such as PostNL, DHL and DPD and links easily with webshops, so you can create shipping labels automatically and benefit from competitive rates.</p>
<p>WooCommerce, on the other hand, is a popular e-commerce plugin for WordPress that lets you set up and manage an online store. With WooCommerce you can add products, accept payments and track and process your customers' orders.</p>
<h3>An introduction to Sendy</h3>
<p>Sendy takes care of the shipping side of your business. It gives you access to several carriers in one place, requests a shipping label automatically based on the details of an order, and sends the track and trace information back so you and your customers always know where a parcel is. That makes Sendy a handy way to streamline your shipping process.</p>
<h3>An introduction to WooCommerce</h3>
<p>WooCommerce is a powerful tool for setting up and managing an online store. With WooCommerce you can easily add products, upload images, set prices and handle inventory management. It also offers flexible shipping options and integrations with popular payment gateways, so you can provide a seamless shopping experience for your customers.</p>
