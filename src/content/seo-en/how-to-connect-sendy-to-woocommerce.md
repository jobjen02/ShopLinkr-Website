<p>Sendy and WooCommerce don't talk to each other directly. ShopLinkr sits in between: you connect both your WooCommerce store and <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/integrations/sendy">Sendy</a> to ShopLinkr, and from there you process your orders and print your shipping labels in one place. WooCommerce brings in the orders, Sendy handles the shipping, and ShopLinkr links the two. Here is exactly how to connect Sendy to WooCommerce.</p>

<h2>How the connection works</h2>
<p>WooCommerce is your <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/features/orders">sales channel</a> and Sendy is a shipping solution that plugs into carriers. ShopLinkr is the order processing platform in the middle. Once both are connected, new WooCommerce orders flow into ShopLinkr automatically. You request the matching label per order without retyping addresses, and the track &amp; trace is sent back to WooCommerce so your customer gets a shipping notification.</p>

<h2>Connect Sendy to WooCommerce in 5 steps</h2>
<ol>
<li><p>Create a ShopLinkr account.</p></li>
<li><p>Connect your WooCommerce store as a sales channel. Your open orders are imported.</p></li>
<li><p>Add Sendy as a <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/features/carriers">carrier</a> in ShopLinkr.</p></li>
<li><p>Open an order and request the Sendy shipping label straight from ShopLinkr. With AutoPrint, the label rolls out of your printer automatically.</p></li>
<li><p>The track &amp; trace goes back to WooCommerce and your customer is notified.</p></li>
</ol>
