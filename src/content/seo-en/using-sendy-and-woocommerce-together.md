<p>In this article we cover how to use Sendy and WooCommerce together. Sendy is a <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/blog/connect-sendy-to-shoplinkr">shipping platform that lets you create labels</a> for carriers such as PostNL. Let's take a look at what Sendy and WooCommerce actually are and how you can use them.</p>
<h2>What is Sendy and how does it work?</h2>
<p>Sendy is a powerful shipping platform built specifically for e-commerce businesses. It lets you easily create labels and shipping documents for various carriers, such as PostNL, DHL and UPS. With Sendy you can also <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/integrations/sendy">calculate shipping costs based on different parameters</a>, such as weight, dimensions and destination.</p>
<p>The benefits of Sendy include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Faster and more efficient shipping of your parcels</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The ability to calculate shipping costs accurately</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Automation of the shipping process, which saves you time</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>To install Sendy, you need to go to the Sendy website and follow the instructions to install it on your server. After installation, you can log in to your Sendy dashboard and start setting up your carriers and sales channels.</p>
<p>Sendy also offers advanced features such as tracking shipments in real time and generating reports about your shipping activity. This allows you to analyze the performance of your shipments and identify any bottlenecks in your logistics process.</p>
<p>In addition, Sendy has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to manage and track shipments. You can easily add new shipments, print shipping labels and check the status of your parcels, all from one central dashboard.</p>
<h2>What is WooCommerce and how does it work?</h2>
<p>WooCommerce is a free plugin for WordPress that lets you build a professional webshop. It offers plenty of features, such as product management, shopping carts and payment gateways. With WooCommerce you can also manage the shipping methods and shipping costs for your customers.</p>
<p>The benefits of WooCommerce include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Easy installation and configuration</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Flexibility and customizability</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Built-in marketing tools</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>To install WooCommerce, you need to go to the WordPress Plugin Directory and download and activate the plugin. After installation, you can adjust the WooCommerce settings and add your products to your webshop.</p>
<p>WooCommerce is popular not only for its ease of use, but also because of the large community behind it. Thousands of themes and extensions are available that can expand the functionality of your webshop. Whether you sell digital products, physical goods or even services, WooCommerce lets you create a tailored shopping experience for your customers.</p>
<p>Another benefit of WooCommerce is its seamless integration with various payment gateways, including PayPal, Stripe and Authorize.Net. This means your customers can pay for their purchases safely and easily, which benefits conversion and customer satisfaction. On top of that, WooCommerce offers extensive reporting tools that give you insight into your webshop's performance, such as sales figures, popular products and customer behavior.</p>
<h2>Integrating Sendy and WooCommerce</h2>
<p>By integrating Sendy and WooCommerce, you can benefit from the strengths of both platforms. One of the main benefits of integration is that you can connect carriers and sales channels to ShopLinkr, a feature of Sendy. This lets you process orders from a sales channel using a carrier's labels.</p>
<p>The benefits of integration include:</p>
<ul>

<p>Seamless integration of the shipping process with your webshop</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The ability to automatically calculate shipping costs and add them to the cart</p>

<p>Tracking options for your customers</p>

<p>To integrate Sendy and WooCommerce, you need to install and activate the Sendy WooCommerce Plugin. After that, you can configure the necessary settings in the WooCommerce Dashboard, such as the Sendy API key and the carriers and sales channels you want to use. Once the integration is complete, your webshop's orders are automatically synchronized with Sendy and you can create labels for your carriers.</p>

This streamlined communication contributes to a better customer experience and more efficient business processes.</p>
<h2>Common problems and solutions</h2>
<p>Using Sendy and WooCommerce together can sometimes bring up problems. A common issue, for example, is that shipping costs are not calculated correctly. This can be caused by incorrect settings in WooCommerce or incorrect data in Sendy. To solve this problem, you need to make sure the settings in both platforms are configured correctly and that the data has been entered correctly.</p>
<p>Solutions are also available for other common problems, such as issues synchronizing orders between Sendy and WooCommerce or being unable to generate labels. Consult the documentation for Sendy and the plugin for possible solutions to specific problems.</p>
