<p>Want to connect DPD to Shopify without creating a label by hand for every order? With ShopLinkr you pull your Shopify orders in automatically, print a DPD shipping label in one click and send the track & trace straight back to your store. <h2>Why connect DPD and Shopify through ShopLinkr</h2>
<p>Shopify does not create DPD labels on its own. Without a connection you retype address details in the DPD portal and paste the tracking back into your order by hand. That costs time and invites typos. ShopLinkr takes that step off your plate:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your <a href="/en/features/orders">Shopify orders</a> come in automatically, alongside channels like bol, WooCommerce and Kaufland.</li>
<li>You create a DPD label in one click, or fully automatically with AutoPrint as soon as an order is ready.</li>
<li>The track & trace is sent back to Shopify automatically, so your customer gets a shipping email right away.</li>
</ul>
<p>Because everything runs centrally, you can combine DPD with other <a href="/en/features/carriers">carriers</a> like PostNL and MyParcel and pick the right one per order.</p>

<h2>Step by step: connecting DPD to Shopify</h2>
<p>You set the connection up once, then shipping runs on its own:</p>
<ol>
<li>Create a ShopLinkr account and connect your Shopify store as a sales channel.</li>
<li>Add DPD as a carrier and enter your DPD account details.</li>
<li>Set which DPD shipping method is used by default and, if you like, link your label printer to AutoPrint.</li>
<li>Process a test order, check the label and the track & trace, then switch on automatic processing.</li>
</ol>
<p>Handling larger volumes? With <a href="/en/features/pick-lists">pick lists</a> you walk a smart route through your warehouse, scan products at the bin and print the DPD label right at the packing station. Picking, packing and shipping become one continuous flow.</p>

<h2>No more slip-ups in stock and shipping</h2>
