<p>Want to connect DPD to WooCommerce so you can ship parcels fast and with track & trace? The simplest route is to put an order processing system between your store and the carrier. ShopLinkr pulls in your WooCommerce orders, lets you print DPD labels with one click, and sends the track & trace back to your store automatically so your customer can follow their parcel. Here's how it works.</p>

<h2>Connecting DPD to WooCommerce through ShopLinkr</h2>
<p>WooCommerce is an e-commerce plugin for WordPress, and DPD is a parcel carrier. By default they stand apart, so you create each shipping label by hand in the DPD portal. Linking your store and your carrier through ShopLinkr removes that retyping. Here's how to set it up:</p>
<ol>
<li>Create a ShopLinkr account and connect your WooCommerce store so your orders flow in.</li>
<li>Add DPD as a <a href="/functionaliteiten/vervoerders">carrier</a> in ShopLinkr.</li>
<li>Open an order, pick DPD, and print the shipping label with one click. With AutoPrint the label comes straight out of your label printer.</li>
<li>The track & trace is sent back to WooCommerce automatically, so your customer gets their tracking link without you lifting a finger.</li>
</ol>
<p>If you sell across multiple channels, everything lands in one place. ShopLinkr also processes orders from bol, Shopify, Kaufland, and other marketplaces, so you don't have to switch tools per channel.</p>

<h2>Why a middle layer beats a standalone plugin</h2>
<p>A standalone DPD plugin only handles the label. The rest of your shipping process stays manual. With an order processing system in between, you get more done in a single move:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>No overselling.</strong> Your <a href="/functionaliteiten/voorraad">inventory</a> syncs in real time across every channel, so a sold item drops everywhere at once.</li>
<li><strong>Faster picking.</strong> With <a href="/functionaliteiten/picklijsten">pick lists</a> you follow a smart route through your warehouse and scan products with a barcode scanner at the packing station.</li>
<li><strong>One screen for everything.</strong> Your <a href="/functionaliteiten/bestellingen">orders</a> from all channels sit together, including returns and status.</li>
</ul>
<p>That way printing the label becomes the final step of a process that already runs smoothly, instead of a separate chore.</p>

<h2>Frequently asked questions</h2>
<h3>Do I still need a separate DPD plugin?</h3>
<p>No. Once you connect WooCommerce and DPD through ShopLinkr, you create your labels in ShopLinkr. There's no separate shipping plugin to install or maintain in WooCommerce.</p>
<h3>Does the track & trace reach my customer automatically?</h3>
<p>Yes. After you create the label, ShopLinkr sends the track & trace back to your WooCommerce order, which then goes out in the standard status email to your customer.</p>
<h3>Can I use carriers other than DPD?</h3>
<p>Yes. Alongside DPD, ShopLinkr works with PostNL, MyParcel, Sendcloud, and more. You choose the carrier per order.</p>
