<p>If you sell on bol, you want orders out the door fast and without hassle. By <strong>connecting Sendcloud to bol</strong> you pull your bol orders in automatically, print shipping labels in a few clicks, and send track & trace back to the customer. Here is exactly how to set up the connection and what you need.</p>

<h2>Connect Sendcloud to bol in 4 steps</h2>
<p>Sendcloud is a shipping platform for creating labels and tracking shipments; bol is a large Dutch and Belgian sales channel. You set up the link from the Sendcloud dashboard:</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Create a Sendcloud account if you do not have one yet.</p></li>
<li><p>In Sendcloud, open the sales channels overview and choose to add a channel.</p></li>
<li><p>Select bol from the list and log in to your bol seller account to grant Sendcloud permission.</p></li>
<li><p>Confirm the connection. Your bol orders then show up in Sendcloud automatically, ready to process.</p></li>
</ol>
<p>From there, Sendcloud imports your orders, you pick a carrier such as PostNL or DPD, and you print the label. The track & trace goes back to bol automatically, so your customer can follow the shipment.</p>

<h2>Why the connection saves you time</h2>
<p>Without a connection, you retype every address by hand and paste track & trace codes back into bol one by one. The link removes that retyping. You process several orders in a batch, cut down on wrong addresses, and your customer gets a shipping notice sooner. To streamline shipping further, it helps to manage your carriers in one place alongside your orders and inventory. Take a look at <a href="/en/features/carriers">carriers and shipping labels</a> in ShopLinkr.</p>

<h2>Frequently asked questions</h2>
<h3>Do I need a bol API key?</h3>
<p>To set up the link, you authorize Sendcloud through your bol seller account. Keep your login details handy; depending on the step, Sendcloud asks for permission or for bol API credentials. The on-screen steps walk you through it.</p>
<h3>Can I connect multiple bol accounts?</h3>
<p>Yes. Add each bol account separately as a sales channel in Sendcloud and authorize them one by one. After that, you manage all orders from a single dashboard.</p>
<h3>How do I test that the connection works?</h3>
<p>Place a test order on bol and process it in Sendcloud. Check that the label is generated correctly and that the track & trace makes it back to bol.</p>
