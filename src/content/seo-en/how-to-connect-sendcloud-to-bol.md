<h2>Step-by-step guide to connecting Sendcloud to bol</h2>
<p>Before you can connect Sendcloud to bol, you need to make a few preparations. Below you will find a step-by-step guide to help you connect Sendcloud to bol:</p>
<h3>Preparing for the connection</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Make sure you have a Sendcloud account. If you do not have an account yet, you can create one for free on the Sendcloud website.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Go to your Sendcloud dashboard and click "Sales channels" in the menu on the left.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Add" and select "bol.com" from the list of sales channels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will now be redirected to the bol.com website. Log in to your bol seller account and give Sendcloud permission to connect to your account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>After you have given permission, you will be sent back to your Sendcloud dashboard. You will now see that bol.com has been successfully added as a sales channel.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>The connection process</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to your Sendcloud dashboard and click "Sales channels".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "bol.com" and then "Connect".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the connection. Among other things, you need to enter your bol.com API key to authorize the connection.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Once the connection is complete, you can adjust the bol.com settings to your liking. For example, you can configure the shipping options and customize the shipping labels.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<p>If you notice that problems occur after connecting Sendcloud to bol, you can follow these steps to solve the problem:</p>

</li>
