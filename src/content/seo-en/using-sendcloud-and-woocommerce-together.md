<h2>What is Sendcloud?</h2>
<p>Sendcloud is a shipping platform designed specifically to optimize the shipping process for webshops. With Sendcloud you can connect various carriers to your sales channels, such as WooCommerce. This lets you manage all your orders from one central place, without hassle or mistakes.</p>
<h3>The benefits of Sendcloud</h3>
<p>Sendcloud offers several benefits for online retailers. First of all, it saves time and effort. You no longer have to create shipping labels manually or sign separate contracts with carriers. On top of that, Sendcloud offers attractive shipping rates thanks to the combined purchasing power of thousands of webshops. This means you can save money on your shipping costs.</p>
<p>Another benefit of Sendcloud is automation. You can set up rules to streamline the shipping process, such as automatically selecting the right carrier based on the weight and destination of the order. This saves time and helps prevent mistakes. In addition, Sendcloud offers extensive tracking options, so that you and your customers are always aware of the shipping status.</p>

<h2>What is WooCommerce?</h2>
<p>WooCommerce is a popular e-commerce plugin that lets you build a fully functional webshop on top of WordPress. With WooCommerce you can add products, accept payments and manage orders.</p>
<h3>The benefits of WooCommerce</h3>
<p>WooCommerce has many benefits for online retailers. It is user-friendly and easy to install. You do not need any technical knowledge to create a nice and professional webshop.</p>
<p>In addition, WooCommerce offers countless options for extension. You can add plugins to add functionality, such as managing inventory, offering discount codes and integrating payment methods.</p>
<p>WooCommerce is also highly flexible and scalable. It can be tailored to the specific needs of your webshop and can grow as your business grows.</p>
<h3>How does WooCommerce work?</h3>
<p>With WooCommerce you can easily add products to your webshop. You can add images, descriptions, prices and other product details. You can also use various categories and tags to organize your products.</p>
<p>When customers want to buy a product, they can add it to their shopping cart and check out using the payment method of their choice. You then receive a notification of the order and can manage it through the WooCommerce dashboard.</p>
