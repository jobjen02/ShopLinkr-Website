<p>Selling on bol.com gives you access to a large Dutch sales channel with plenty of buyers. You create a seller account, list your products on the platform, and then handle orders, inventory, and shipping. Below is how to get started, and how to keep it manageable with <a href="/integraties">ShopLinkr</a>, even when you sell on other channels alongside bol.com.</p>

<h2>Creating a seller account</h2>
<p>To sell as a business, you need a Chamber of Commerce registration. After that, you work through these steps:</p>
<ol>
<li>Go to bol.com and register as a seller.</li>
<li>Enter your business and bank details and confirm your account.</li>
<li>Add your first products with a clear title, description, and good photos.</li>
<li>Set your stock, price, and delivery time, then publish the listings.</li>
</ol>
<p>Solid product information is your most important sales tool. A sharp title, complete specifications, and clear photos decide whether a buyer picks your offer.</p>
