<h2>How the connection works through ShopLinkr</h2>
<p>ShopLinkr is the link between your webshop and your carrier. Orders from WooCommerce flow in, you process them from a <a href="/en/features/pick-lists">pick list</a> with a smart route through your warehouse, and you print the QLS label right away. No more retyping labels by hand or copying orders between systems.</p>
<p>As soon as the label is created, ShopLinkr sends the track and trace code back to WooCommerce automatically. Your customer sees the shipping status in their account and confirmation email without you lifting a finger. If you sell across more channels, ShopLinkr also keeps your <a href="/en/features/inventory">inventory</a> in sync in real time, so you never sell stock you no longer have.</p>

</ol>
