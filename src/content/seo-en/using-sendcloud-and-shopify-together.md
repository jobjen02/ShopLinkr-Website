<h2>What is Sendcloud?</h2>
<p>Sendcloud is a powerful <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/blog/connect-sendcloud-to-shoplinkr">shipping platform that simplifies the entire process</a> of sending parcels. <h3>The benefits of Sendcloud</h3>
<p>Sendcloud offers countless benefits for online sellers. First of all, you save time and effort because you can automate all your shipping processes. With Sendcloud you can easily create bulk shipments, print labels and track shipping statuses. This lets you spend more time on your business and less on logistical hassle.</p>
<p>Another major benefit is the choice of more than 25 carriers. Sendcloud works with renowned carriers such as PostNL, DHL, UPS and FedEx. This means you can get lower shipping costs, which results in higher profit margins.</p>
<p>Sendcloud also offers advanced features such as returns management and tracking notifications. With returns management you can easily handle returns and refunds, which improves your customer service. Tracking notifications keep your customers informed about the status of their orders, ensuring a better customer experience.</p>
<h3>How does Sendcloud work?</h3>
<p>To use Sendcloud, all you have to do is create an account on their website and integrate your Shopify store. This can be done quickly and easily using the Sendcloud app in the Shopify App Store. Once you have integrated your store, all your orders are automatically synced with Sendcloud.</p>
<p>When an order comes in, you can easily create and print shipping labels for the carrier you have chosen. After that, you can track the order with the tracking information and send notifications to your customers. Sendcloud also offers an advanced dashboard where you have full control over your shipping processes and can generate reports.</p>

<h3>How does Shopify work?</h3>

<p>Shopify offers a <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/en/blog/connect-shopify-to-shoplinkr">user-friendly interface</a> where you can add your products, set prices, write product descriptions and upload images.
