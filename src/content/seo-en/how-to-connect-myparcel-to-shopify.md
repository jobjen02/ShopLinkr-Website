<li><p>Add Shopify as a sales channel so your orders flow into ShopLinkr automatically.</p></li>
<li><p>Connect MyParcel as a carrier in your settings.</p></li>
<li><p>Open an incoming Shopify order and create a MyParcel label in one click.</p></li>
</ol>

<h2>Frequently asked questions</h2>
<h3>Do I need to connect MyParcel to Shopify separately?</h3>
<p>No. You connect both Shopify and MyParcel to ShopLinkr. ShopLinkr pulls in the orders from Shopify, creates the MyParcel labels, and sends the track &amp; trace back. You don't need a separate link between Shopify and MyParcel.</p>
<h3>Can I use other carriers too?</h3>
<p>Yes. Alongside MyParcel you can connect PostNL, DPD or Sendcloud, for example. You choose which carrier to use per order or through an automation rule.</p>
<h3>Does the track & trace show up in Shopify automatically?</h3>
<p>Yes. As soon as you create a label, ShopLinkr sends the track &amp; trace code back to Shopify, so your customer gets the status update automatically.</p>
