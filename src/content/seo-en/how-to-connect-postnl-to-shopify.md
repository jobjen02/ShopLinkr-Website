<h2>Step-by-step guide to connecting PostNL to Shopify</h2>
<p>To connect PostNL to Shopify, follow these simple steps:</p>
<h3>Log in to your Shopify account</h3>
<p>Navigate to the Shopify login page and enter your login details. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one by filling in the required information.</p>
<h3>Installing the PostNL app</h3>
<p>Go to the Shopify App Store and search for the PostNL app. Click 'Install' and follow the instructions to add the app to your Shopify store.</p>
<h3>Configuring the shipping settings</h3>
<p>After installing the PostNL app, go to the 'Shipping' section in your Shopify admin. Select 'PostNL' as the carrier and configure your shipping rates and rules as desired. Don't forget to set the correct weight and dimension units.</p>
<p>To make sure the connection between PostNL and Shopify runs smoothly, it is important to also check the customs settings. Make sure you have all the necessary customs documents and information ready for international shipments. PostNL offers support and guidance with customs formalities, but good preparation on your part can prevent any delays.</p>
<p>In addition, it is wise to explore PostNL's tracking options and integrate them into your Shopify store. Customers appreciate being able to follow their orders from the moment of shipment all the way to delivery. By offering tracking functionality, you improve customer satisfaction and trust in your webshop. PostNL provides detailed tracking information, including the expected delivery date and any status updates during the delivery process.</p>
