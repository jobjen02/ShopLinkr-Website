<p>Are you an online seller looking to make your shipping process more efficient? Then using QLS and Bol together is the perfect solution for you! In this article we cover the basics of QLS and Bol, as well as the benefits of using both platforms. We will also walk through the integration process, common problems and their solutions, and a few handy tips to get the most out of QLS and Bol. </p>
<h2>What is QLS?</h2>
<p>QLS is a shipping platform that makes it easy to <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/blogs/qls-koppelen-aan-shoplinkr">create shipping labels</a> and streamline your shipping process. It lets you link carriers and sales channels together through the ShopLinkr functionality. This way you can process orders from a sales channel using the labels of a carrier. QLS supports carriers such as PostNL, DHL, FedEx and more. With QLS you no longer have to create shipping labels by hand, and the entire process is automated.</p>
<h3>The basics of QLS</h3>
<p>To use QLS, you first need to connect your <a href="https://shoplinkr.com/koppelingen/qls">sales channels and carriers</a> to the platform. Once you have set up this connection, you can easily generate shipping labels for your orders through QLS. The system automatically retrieves the information it needs, such as the delivery address and the weight of the package. This saves you time and makes sure your shipping labels are accurate.</p>
<h3>The benefits of using QLS</h3>
<p>By using QLS, you enjoy several benefits. First, you save time by automating the shipping process. You no longer have to create shipping labels by hand and can spend your time on other important tasks for your business. On top of that, QLS reduces the chance of errors, because the system retrieves the required information automatically. This lowers the risk of incorrect shipping labels and returns. Moreover, QLS lets you work with different carriers, giving you flexibility and making sure you can always pick the best shipping option for your business.</p>
<h2>What is Bol?</h2>
<p>Bol is one of the largest online marketplaces in the Netherlands and Belgium. It allows sellers to offer their products to millions of potential customers. By integrating Bol with QLS, you can optimize your shipping process even further.</p>
<h3>The basics of Bol</h3>
<p>To be able to use Bol, you have to create a seller account. Once you have added your products to your Bol seller account, potential customers can find and order your products. As a seller, you are responsible for packing and shipping the orders to your customers. Integrating Bol with QLS makes this process much more efficient and streamlined.</p>
<h3>The benefits of using Bol</h3>
<p>Using Bol offers many benefits for sellers. First, you reach an enormous customer base, because Bol is a popular and trusted marketplace. You can take advantage of Bol's brand recognition and attract more potential customers. In addition, Bol offers an advanced logistics service called "<a href="https://shoplinkr.com/blogs/bol-com-koppelen-aan-shoplinkr">Fulfillment by Bol</a>" (FBB). With FBB you can store your stock in Bol's warehouse, and they will take care of shipping and handling your orders. By integrating Bol with QLS, you can handle the shipping process for FBB orders efficiently.</p>

<h3>The benefits of integrating QLS and Bol</h3>
<p>By using QLS and Bol together, you enjoy several benefits. First, your orders from Bol are automatically synchronized with QLS, so you no longer have to spend time entering order information by hand. On top of that, you can easily generate the shipping labels through QLS, streamlining the shipping process even further. By integrating QLS and Bol, you make your shipping process more efficient and create a seamless collaboration between both platforms.</p>
