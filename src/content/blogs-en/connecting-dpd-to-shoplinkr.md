<p><strong>Want to give your shipping process a serious upgrade? Good news! The DPD integration in ShopLinkr makes shipping a lot easier. You can connect your own DPD contract to ShopLinkr and take advantage of handy shipping options.</strong></p>
<p>Check out the guide for connecting DPD <a href="https://support.shoplinkr.com/artikel/dpd-koppelen" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<h2>No more hassle</h2>
<p>Manual shipping processes are a thing of the past. With the DPD integration you can handle everything from one place. Connect your DPD contract and manage your shipments with ease.</p>
<h2>Your rates, your choice</h2>
<p>The great thing about this integration? You can simply use your existing DPD rates. No extra hassle adjusting prices, your customers get the same rates as always.</p>
<h2>Labels in a snap</h2>
<p>Creating shipping labels has never been this easy. Thanks to the DPD integration you generate shipping labels in no time. No more time-consuming work.</p>
<h2>Always in the know</h2>
<p>With real-time tracking you can follow your shipments every step of the way. Both you and your customers always know where an order is.</p>
<h2>All-in-one system</h2>
<p>The DPD integration makes your life easier. You have everything in one place: orders, inventory and shipments. That means fewer mistakes and more overview.</p>
