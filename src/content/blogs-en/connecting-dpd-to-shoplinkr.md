<p><strong>With the DPD integration in ShopLinkr you ship your orders using your own DPD contract, without switching between systems. You create labels, print them and track shipments from the same place where you manage your orders and inventory.</strong></p>
<p>Check out the guide for connecting DPD <a href="/en/support/connect-dpd">here</a>.</p>
<h2>Your own DPD contract, your own rates</h2>
<p>You connect the DPD contract you already have to ShopLinkr, so you keep your own agreements and rates with nothing to renegotiate or set up again. DPD is one of the <a href="/en/features/carriers">carriers</a> ShopLinkr supports, alongside PostNL, MyParcel and Sendcloud among others, so you can expand later without changing the way you work.</p>
<h2>One-click labels and automatic track & trace</h2>
<p>For each order you create a DPD shipping label with one click. With AutoPrint your labels go straight to your printer, so you can handle larger batches in a row. As soon as a label is created, ShopLinkr sends the track & trace code back to the sales channel the order came from, such as bol, Shopify or WooCommerce. You and your customer can follow the shipment without passing anything along by hand.</p>
<h2>Shipping as part of your order process</h2>
<p>The DPD integration does not stand on its own. Shipping is the last step in a process that starts at your <a href="/en/features/orders">orders</a> and runs through <a href="/en/features/pick-lists">pick lists</a> with a smart warehouse route and barcode scanning. Orders, inventory and shipments live in one system, so you switch between tools less and make fewer mistakes.</p>
<h2>Get started with DPD in ShopLinkr</h2>
<p>Connect your DPD contract and ship your first parcels without a separate shipping tool. <a href="https://app.shoplinkr.com/auth/register">Create an account</a> and try ShopLinkr 14 days free. Take a look at the other <a href="/en/integrations">integrations</a> to tie your sales channels and carriers together.</p>
