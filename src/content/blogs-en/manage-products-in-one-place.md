<p>Do you work with multiple sales channels? Then you probably know the feeling: your products are scattered everywhere, and you quickly lose track of things. That is why, in ShopLinkr, we are building one central product catalog in which you can see all your items neatly lined up.</p>
<p>For each product you can see at a glance what the current stock is, which location it is stored at, which tags are attached to it and which channels it is linked with. Does something change in your stock? Then ShopLinkr keeps your stock levels in sync across channels in real time, so you never again sell something that is no longer on the shelf.</p>
