<p>If you sell on bol and on your own webshop or another platform, you share the same physical stock across multiple channels. The risk is in the gap: when an item sells on bol while Shopify still shows it as available, you sell something you no longer have. The result is a cancellation and a disappointed customer. Stock synchronization fixes this by keeping your numbers the same everywhere.</p>

<h2>What stock synchronization does</h2>
<p>Synchronization means a sale on one channel instantly adjusts the stock on every other channel. Sell a product on bol, and the count also drops on your webshop and other marketplaces. You work from a single stock count instead of separate numbers per platform that you try to keep up to date by hand.</p>
<p>ShopLinkr processes the orders from all your channels centrally and keeps stock <a href="/en/features/inventory">in real-time sync</a>. Alongside bol you can connect Shopify, WooCommerce and Kaufland, so everything runs off the same numbers.</p>

<h2>Why this matters</h2>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>No overselling</strong>: you avoid selling something that is already gone, and with it the cancellations and negative reviews on bol.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Less manual work</strong>: updating counts per channel by hand takes time and invites mistakes. Automatic sync takes that off your plate.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Room for more channels</strong>: once your stock keeps itself up to date, you can safely sell on extra platforms without losing track.</p></li>
</ul>
