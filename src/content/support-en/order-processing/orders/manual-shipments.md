<p>With manual shipments you can create a shipment that is not linked to an order from your sales channel.</p>
<h3>Creating a manual shipment</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Shipments</strong> and click <strong>Create shipment</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the <strong>customer details</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the <strong>delivery address</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose the <strong>shipping option</strong> you want to ship with.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the weight if applicable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Create shipment</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>A shipping label is generated automatically and opens right away so you can print it.</p>
