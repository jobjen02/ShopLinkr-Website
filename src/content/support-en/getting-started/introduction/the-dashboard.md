<p>When you log in to ShopLinkr, you land on the dashboard. Here you can see at a glance how your business is doing. The dashboard is made up of two parts: the <strong>main overview</strong> and the <strong>insights</strong>.</p>
<h3>Main overview</h3>
<p>At the top of the dashboard you'll see five cards with today's most important figures:</p>

<h3>Insights</h3>
<p>Below the main overview you'll find detailed insights for a period of your choosing. Every figure is compared with the previous period, so you can immediately see whether you're growing or declining.</p>
