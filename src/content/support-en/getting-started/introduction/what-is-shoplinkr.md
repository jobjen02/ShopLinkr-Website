<p>ShopLinkr is a WMS (warehouse management software) for webshops that lets you run your entire order processing, inventory management and shipping from a single platform. Whether you sell through your own webshop or through several sales channels at once, ShopLinkr brings everything together in one central place.</p>
<h3>Processing orders</h3>
<p>All your orders from all your sales channels come in automatically in ShopLinkr. From there you can process, <a href="/support/wat-zijn-picklijsten">pick</a>, pack and ship them. You decide how you work: with or without <a href="/support/bakken-voor-picken">bins</a>, with a handheld scanner or manually, and with one or more <a href="/support/inpakstations-instellen">packing stations</a>.</p>
<h3>Managing inventory</h3>
<p>Track your inventory in real time across all your sales channels. ShopLinkr syncs your stock automatically, so you never sell more than you have in stock. You can work with <a href="/support/locatiebeheer">warehouse locations</a>, carry out <a href="/support/voorraadtellingen">stock counts</a> and set up <a href="/support/voorraad-notificaties">notifications</a> when a product drops below a certain stock threshold.</p>
<h3>Connecting multiple sales channels</h3>
<p>Easily connect your webshops and marketplaces such as <a href="/support/shopify-koppelen">Shopify</a>, <a href="/support/woocommerce-koppelen">WooCommerce</a>, <a href="/support/bol-com-koppelen">Bol</a> and <a href="/support/kaufland-koppelen">Kaufland</a>. Your products are imported into ShopLinkr and orders and inventory are synced automatically. That way you manage your warehouse from ShopLinkr and no longer have to log in to each platform separately.</p>
<h3>Shipping with your favourite carrier</h3>
<p>ShopLinkr integrates with carriers such as <a href="/support/sendcloud-koppelen">Sendcloud</a>, <a href="/support/myparcel-koppelen">MyParcel</a>, <a href="/support/dpd-koppelen">DPD</a> and more. Generate shipping labels and print them automatically with <a href="/support/auto-print-instellen">AutoPrint</a>. You can choose the best shipping option per order, or have it done automatically with <a href="/support/wat-zijn-regels">rules</a>.</p>
<h3>Insight into your performance</h3>
<p>View <a href="/support/rapporten-overzicht">reports</a> on your product performance, inventory value and more. Discover which products sell best, which products never sell and how to optimise your warehouse layout with the <a href="/support/abc-analyse">ABC analysis</a>.</p>
<h3>Smart automation with rules</h3>
<p>Set up <a href="/support/wat-zijn-regels">rules</a> that automatically carry out actions when a new order comes in. Think of automatically assigning a carrier, adding <a href="/support/tags-beheren">tags</a>, flagging an order as high priority or postponing orders to the Wadden Islands.</p>
<h3>Working together with your team</h3>
<p>Working with more than one person? <a href="/support/gebruikers-uitnodigen">Invite colleagues</a> and use <a href="/support/rollen-en-rechten-beheren">roles and permissions</a> to define exactly what everyone is allowed to do. Warehouse staff can log in quickly with a <a href="/support/inloggen-met-qr-code">QR code</a> and PIN.</p>
<h3>Who is ShopLinkr for?</h3>
<p>ShopLinkr is built specifically for webshops with their own warehouse. Whether you are a small business that is just starting to grow, or a larger company with multiple sales channels: ShopLinkr scales with you thanks to the flexible <a href="/support/het-pay-as-you-go-model">Pay as you Go model</a>.</p>
