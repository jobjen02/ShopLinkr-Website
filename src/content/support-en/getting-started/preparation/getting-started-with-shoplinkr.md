<p>As soon as you have started the free trial, you can begin using ShopLinkr right away. You have access to all features.</p>
<p>The best first step is to connect a sales channel. This gives you instant access to all of that channel's products and stock.</p>
<h3>Connect your sales channel</h3>
<p>To connect your sales channel, go to <a href="/support/categorieen/integraties/webshops-en-marketplaces">this page</a>. It contains all the articles on how to connect your sales channel. Once you have connected a sales channel, your products and their stock will appear in ShopLinkr. This process can take up to about 30 minutes, depending on the number of products you have and the type of sales channel. Once everything is loaded, ShopLinkr becomes the place to keep track of all your stock.</p>
<h3>Connect your carrier</h3>
<p>With a carrier connected, you can process orders directly from ShopLinkr. You can then choose a shipping option per order (or in bulk) and process those orders. Shipping labels are then created with the chosen carrier. These labels are immediately available with the order and can easily be opened or printed (in bulk) using your (label) printer. Go to <a href="/support/categorieen/integraties/vervoerders">this page</a> to see how to connect your carrier.</p>
