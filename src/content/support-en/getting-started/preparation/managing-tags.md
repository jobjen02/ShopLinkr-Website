<p>Tags let you label products and orders so you can find and filter them easily. Think of tags such as "Fragile", "Sale" or "Christmas". Each tag has a name and a color, so you recognize them at a glance.</p>
<h3>Two types of tags</h3>
<p>ShopLinkr has two types of tags:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Product tags</strong>: use these to categorize products. For example, to mark seasonal products, identify products that are being phased out, or label products that need to be packed with extra care.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Order tags</strong>: use these to visually distinguish orders. For example, to mark large orders, label orders that will be picked up, or recognize orders with gift wrapping.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Creating a tag</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Tags</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Add tag</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter a <strong>name</strong> (maximum 30 characters).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose a <strong>color</strong> so the tag stands out visually.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>type</strong>: product or order.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>Assigning tags</h3>
<p>You can assign tags manually to a product or order. Open the item and add the tag or tags you want. A product or order can have several tags at the same time.</p>
<h3>Assigning tags automatically with rules</h3>
<p>With <a href="/en/support/what-are-rules">rules</a> you can assign tags automatically to new orders. For example, set up a rule that automatically gives every order above a certain amount the tag "Large". That way you do not have to do it by hand.</p>
<h3>Filtering by tags</h3>
<p>You can filter by tags on both the products page and the orders page. This is handy for quickly finding a specific group of items.</p>
<h3>Tags in reports</h3>
<p>When generating <a href="/en/support/reports-overview">reports</a> you can filter by tags. This lets you, for example, review the performance of just your seasonal products.</p>
