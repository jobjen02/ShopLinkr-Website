<p>The product performance report shows you at a glance how each product is doing. For every product you can review the revenue, profit, margin, costs and returns over a period you choose yourself. This way you know exactly which products are selling well and where your margins are under pressure.</p>

<h3>Exporting</h3>
<p>Click <strong>Export</strong> to download the report. You receive the file by email with all the data from the report.</p>
<h3>How do you use this report?</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Identify top performers</strong>: sort by revenue or profit to see which products contribute the most to your bottom line.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Spot margin pressure</strong>: products with a low margin may cost you more than they bring in. Consider adjusting the selling price or renegotiating the purchase price.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Flag return issues</strong>: a high number of returns can point to quality problems or unclear product information.</p>
</li>
</ul>
