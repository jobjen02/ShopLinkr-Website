<p>Working with a team? You can easily invite colleagues to collaborate in ShopLinkr. Each user gets their own account with their own email address and password.</p>
<h3>Inviting a user</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Users</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the <strong>Invite user</strong> button.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your colleague's email address.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose the role you want to assign. The role determines which permissions the user receives (see also <a href="/support/rollen-en-rechten-beheren">Managing roles and permissions</a>).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Invite</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Your colleague now receives an email with an invitation link.</p>
<h3>What does the invited person need to do?</h3>
<p>The invited person clicks the link in the email and lands on a registration page. Here they fill in the following details:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>First name and last name</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A password of their choice (at least 8 characters, with uppercase and lowercase letters)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>After accepting the invitation, the new user can log in right away and get started.</p>
<h3>Good to know</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>The email address is already filled in and cannot be changed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The assigned role is automatically linked to the new account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The invitation has no expiry date, but each link can only be used once.</p>
</li>
</ul>
