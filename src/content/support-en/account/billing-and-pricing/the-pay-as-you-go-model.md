<p>We work with a "Pay as you Go" model. This is hugely beneficial and transparent for the customer. You really pay for what you use.</p>
<p>Imagine you are doing well selling across various platforms, but then suddenly one month goes incredibly badly. Your revenue drops by 80% and all of a sudden there is no budget left for an expensive inventory management system. This is where ShopLinkr comes in. ShopLinkr scales along with your number of sales. So if your revenue is 80% lower, ShopLinkr is also a lot cheaper. <a href="/en/pricing">Here</a> you can calculate what the monthly costs will be for your business. The price you see is the price you pay. Nice and fair.</p>
