<p>With QR login, employees can log in quickly by scanning a personal QR code and entering a PIN. This is ideal for warehouse staff who need to switch between accounts fast, for example at a shared <a href="/support/inpakstations-instellen">packing station</a>.</p>
<h3>Enabling QR login</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Security</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Under the <strong>QR login</strong> section, click <strong>Enable</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose a 4-digit PIN.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A personal QR code is generated.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you like, download the badge as a PDF. It contains your name, company name and QR code, so you can print it and hang it up at your workstation.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>How does logging in work?</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to the login screen and choose <strong>Token login</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Scan your personal QR code with the camera.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your 4-digit PIN.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have you enabled <a href="/support/tweefactorauthenticatie-instellen">2FA</a>? Then you will also be asked for your 6-digit code.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You are logged in!</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>Changing your PIN</h3>
<p>You can change your PIN at any time via <strong>Settings &gt; Security &gt; QR login</strong>. Click <strong>Change PIN</strong> and enter your current password to confirm. Then choose a new 4-digit PIN.</p>
<h3>Disabling QR login</h3>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Security &gt; QR login</strong> and click <strong>Disable</strong>. You need to enter your password to confirm. This invalidates your QR code, and it can no longer be used.</p>
