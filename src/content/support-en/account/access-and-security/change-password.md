<p>It is a good idea to change your password regularly. In ShopLinkr you can do this easily through your security settings.</p>
<h3>How to change your password</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Security</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the <strong>Change password</strong> section, start by entering your current password.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Next, enter your new password.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Repeat your new password to confirm it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Update</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Your password has now been changed. The next time you log in, use your new password.</p>
<h3>Password requirements</h3>
<p>Your new password must meet the following requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>At least 8 characters long</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contains both uppercase and lowercase letters</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Forgot your password?</h3>
<p>Have you forgotten your password? On the login screen you can click "Forgot password" to receive a reset link.</p>
