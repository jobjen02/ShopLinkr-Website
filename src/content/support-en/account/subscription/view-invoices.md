<p>You can find all your ShopLinkr invoices in one central place. Handy for your bookkeeping or whenever you want to check an invoice.</p>
<h3>Where do you find your invoices?</h3>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Subscription</strong>. Here you will find the <strong>Invoices</strong> section, with an overview of all your invoices.</p>
<h3>What do you see per invoice?</h3>
<p>For each invoice you see the following information:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Invoice number</strong>: the unique number of the invoice. You can copy this number by clicking on it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Status</strong>: whether the invoice is paid, unpaid, a draft or void.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Total amount</strong>: the total invoice amount.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Date</strong>: the date on which the invoice was created.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>How does the billing work?</h3>
<p>ShopLinkr uses the <a href="/support/het-pay-as-you-go-model">Pay as you Go</a> model. You pay monthly based on your usage. Invoices are created automatically and collected via your linked payment method (SEPA direct debit).</p>
