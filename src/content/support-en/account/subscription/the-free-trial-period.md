<p>When you create an account with ShopLinkr, you automatically get a free trial period. This lets you explore the platform at your own pace, without having to enter any payment details.</p>
<h3>What do you get during the trial period?</h3>
<p>During the trial period you have access to all of ShopLinkr's features. You can connect sales channels, process orders, manage stock and try out everything you need.</p>
<h3>How long does the trial period last?</h3>
<p>The trial period lasts 14 days. You can check the time remaining under <strong>Settings &gt; Subscription</strong>. Here you will see a badge showing the end date of your trial period.</p>
<h3>Starting your subscription during the trial period</h3>
<p>During the trial period you can set up your subscription in advance, so that you can keep working without interruption once the trial ends.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Subscription</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Complete your company details, such as your company name, VAT number and address details.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Add a payment method via SEPA.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Your subscription then starts automatically as soon as the trial period ends. All of your stock, orders and settings are kept just as they were.</p>
<h3>What happens after the trial period?</h3>
<p>Did you start your subscription during the trial period? Then everything continues automatically and you can simply keep working.</p>
<p>Have you not started a subscription yet? Then you can no longer use the platform until you do so. But don't worry: all of your data is kept. As soon as you start your subscription, you can pick up right where you left off.</p>
