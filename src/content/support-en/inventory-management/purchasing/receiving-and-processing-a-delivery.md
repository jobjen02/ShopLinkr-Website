<p>As soon as a delivery from your <a href="/en/support/managing-suppliers">supplier</a> arrives, you can receive the products in ShopLinkr. For each product, you indicate how many you received and which <a href="/en/support/location-management">location</a> you want to store it in. Your stock is updated automatically.</p>
<h3>Mark as purchased</h3>
<p>Before you can receive products, the delivery needs to have the status <strong>Purchased</strong>:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the delivery.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the blue icon in the top right to mark the delivery as purchased.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confirm your choice.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>You will now see the expected products under the heading <strong>Expected</strong>.</p>
<h3>Receiving products</h3>
<p>For each product, you indicate what you received:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click the green icon next to the product.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Indicate whether you <strong>received everything</strong>, or enter the actual quantity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>location</strong> where you want to store the product.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Your stock is updated right away and the product moves from <strong>Expected</strong> to <strong>Received</strong>. You can also use a barcode scanner: ShopLinkr recognizes the product by its EAN code and counts it automatically.</p>
<h3>Partially received</h3>
<p>Received fewer than expected? Enter the actual quantity. The remainder stays listed as expected, so you can still receive it later.</p>
<h3>Undoing a receipt</h3>
<p>Received a product by mistake? You can undo the receipt. The stock is then deducted from the location again.</p>
<h3>Completing the delivery</h3>
<p>Once you are done, click the icon in the top right to mark the delivery as processed. The delivery gets the status <strong>Processed</strong> and can no longer be edited. Products you did not receive are shown under <strong>Not received</strong>, so you can always look back at what was missing.</p>
<h3>Good to know</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Every receipt is recorded as a <a href="/en/support/stock-movements">stock movement</a> with the reason "Delivery received".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If there are <a href="/en/support/backorders">backorders</a> for the received products, they are automatically released as soon as the stock becomes available.</p>
</li>
</ul>
