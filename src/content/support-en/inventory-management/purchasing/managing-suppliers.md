<p>By adding your suppliers in ShopLinkr, you keep a clear overview of who you purchase from. You can link suppliers to products and to <a href="/support/een-levering-aanmaken">deliveries</a>, so you can always see which products you buy from which supplier and which deliveries you have received.</p>
<h3>Adding a supplier</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Settings &gt; Suppliers</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Add supplier</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fill in the details. Only the <strong>company name</strong> is required. In addition, you can enter things such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Contact person, email address and phone number</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>VAT number and Chamber of Commerce number</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Address details and website</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Payment terms and VAT rate</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum order amount</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>Linking suppliers to products</h3>
<p>For each product, you can indicate which supplier(s) you buy it from. A product can be linked to multiple suppliers. Open a product, click <strong>Edit</strong> and select the supplier(s).</p>
<p>Once you have linked suppliers, you can filter by supplier in the <a href="/support/producten-overzicht">product overview</a>. This way you quickly see which products you buy from whom.</p>
<h3>Suppliers and deliveries</h3>
<p>When you <a href="/support/een-levering-aanmaken">create a delivery</a>, you select the supplier the delivery comes from. For each supplier, you can review which deliveries you have received and which are still on their way.</p>
<h3>Purchase advice per supplier</h3>
<p>Want to know how much you need to reorder from a supplier? With the <a href="/support/inkoopadvies">purchase advice</a>, ShopLinkr calculates what you need to buy per supplier based on your stock, backorders and expected sales. You can then turn the advice into concrete deliveries with a single click.</p>
