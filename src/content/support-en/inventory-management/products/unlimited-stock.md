<p>If you sell products that are always available, such as digital products (for example software licenses), there is no need to track stock for them. The <strong>Unlimited stock</strong> feature lets you skip the stock process for these products.</p>
<h3>How do you turn it on?</h3>
<p>Open a product under <strong>Products</strong>, click <strong>Edit</strong>, and enable the <strong>Unlimited stock</strong> option.</p>
<h3>What changes?</h3>
<p>As soon as you enable unlimited stock for a product:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Stock is no longer tracked. No stock is deducted when orders come in.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The product is always reported as available to your sales channels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The product is skipped during <a href="/en/support/stock-counts">stock counts</a>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Sales channels that do not support it</h3>
<p>Some sales platforms do not support the "no stock management" setting. In that case, ShopLinkr automatically sends a stock level of 1000 so the product is not marked as sold out.</p>
<h3>Bundles and unlimited stock</h3>
<p>A bundle automatically gets the unlimited stock status as soon as every product within the bundle has unlimited stock enabled. You do not need to set this manually. As soon as one of the products switches back to normal stock, the bundle loses this status again.</p>
