<p>The import and export feature lets you add products in bulk or download your product data.</p>
<h3>Importing products</h3>
<p>With the import feature you can update products in bulk using a CSV file.</p>
<h4>How do you import products?</h4>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Products</strong> and click <strong>Import products</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Upload your CSV file.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose which field to match your products on: <strong>Product ID</strong> or <strong>EAN code</strong>. This determines how ShopLinkr recognizes and updates existing products.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Map the columns from your file to the right product fields in ShopLinkr. The system tries to do this automatically based on the column names.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Review the summary. If any errors are found, they are shown so you can correct them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong> to start the import.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The import is processed in the background and can take a few minutes, depending on the number of products in your CSV file.</p>
<h3>Exporting products</h3>
<p>Go to <strong>Products</strong> and click <strong>Export products</strong>. Click <strong>Export</strong> to start the export. You will receive an email once the export is ready, containing a CSV file with all your products.</p>
