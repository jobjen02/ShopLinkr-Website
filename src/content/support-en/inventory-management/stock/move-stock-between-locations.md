<p>If you work with multiple locations in your warehouse, you can easily move stock from one location to another. This is handy when, for example, you want to move stock from a bulk location to a pick location.</p>
<h3>Moving stock</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Products</strong> and open the product whose stock you want to move.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Update stock</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>source location</strong>: the location where the stock currently sits.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Set the type to <strong>Move</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>destination location</strong>: the location you want to move the stock to. You can also create a new location right here.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the <strong>quantity</strong> you want to move, or turn on <strong>Move all stock</strong> to move everything at once.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Update</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The stock is moved immediately. You can see the movement in the product's <a href="/en/support/stock-movements">stock movements</a>.</p>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/voorraad-verplaatsen-tussen-locaties/7kzr2WeOKcs5hm3fnnfHILhMzQ.png">
<h3>Good to know</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>You can move at most the number of units available at the source location.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you select the full quantity at the source location, the <strong>Move all stock</strong> toggle is switched on automatically.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Besides moving, you can also manually increase, decrease, or set stock to a specific number from the same screen.</p>
</li>
</ul>
