<p>Follow the steps below to connect your Kaufland account to ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your Kaufland account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Go to "Settings" &gt; "Shop Settings" &gt; "API" and click "Generate new API keys".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/kaufland-koppelen/UDCRP0uu3iunRpocRA3yJRQUg.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Save the "Client key" and "Secret key", as you will need them later.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/kaufland-koppelen/ZYxpaFEyzB629RnBSudrhZWHAkM.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Sales channels" and click "Connect sales channel".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/KZ9zRXIeNiTvaZzNL1FWdLQoOAs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "Kaufland" as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/kaufland-koppelen/zaRQIIjdxR5jt5Z6ncmtHzGnU.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter the "client_key" and "secret_key" that you saved in step 3.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/kaufland-koppelen/WdRDukjV4cAqnFxOz2A7yM4hhs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>If the details you entered are correct, the available countries are retrieved. Select the right country for this sales channel.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/kaufland-koppelen/a9RnNalthaBrpVIkuze7OitbaM8.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>All information is imported and the stock is updated to match the stock in ShopLinkr, provided the product already exists.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to update your stock.</strong></p>
