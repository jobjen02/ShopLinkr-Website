<p>Follow the steps below to connect your WooCommerce webshop to ShopLinkr. To load all products correctly, every product in WooCommerce needs an SKU code.</p>
<h2>Before you start</h2>
<p><strong>If you use the "EAN for WooCommerce" plugin</strong>, you can also enter the EAN codes in WooCommerce. If a product has no EAN code, the SKU field is used as the EAN code in ShopLinkr by default.</p>
<p><strong>Please note:</strong> to make sure the "EAN for WooCommerce" plugin works properly with ShopLinkr, check the following:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>In your WordPress environment, go to "WooCommerce" &gt; "Settings" &gt; "EAN" &gt; "General".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/woocommerce-koppelen/NQFTYwinCDQT9wIEk31EMwXGf50.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Scroll all the way down and make sure the following 2 options are turned on. Do not forget to click "Save changes". This is important, otherwise your products and orders will not be imported correctly.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/woocommerce-koppelen/h5TS0243ondbtkASyKlG15xRWw.png">
<h2>Connecting WooCommerce</h2>
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Sales channels" and click "Connect sales channel".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/KZ9zRXIeNiTvaZzNL1FWdLQoOAs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "WooCommerce" as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/woocommerce-koppelen/GcR6sCwybvvnaguSNNjx8yxAz8.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Under "WooCommerce url", enter the url of your WooCommerce website, for example: https://shoplinkr.com. Please note: some websites require www in combination with https, this differs per WooCommerce site.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/woocommerce-koppelen/ESgFTsbSNT8VdTw2ux5ipEIkGE.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you are not logged in yet, log in now with the admin account of your WordPress environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Approve".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/woocommerce-koppelen/X6DWa4qinbhF7FhAz9qYRkjEh8.png">
<p>All information is imported and the stock is updated to the stock in ShopLinkr, provided the product already exists.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to adjust your stock.</strong></p>
