<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to <strong>Settings &gt; Sales channels</strong> and click <strong>Connect sales channel</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/KZ9zRXIeNiTvaZzNL1FWdLQoOAs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select <strong>bol</strong> as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/KGatCB8xkgTnSYdqlNWLVuY4Byc.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Give your sales channel a suitable name and select the settings you want.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/jzxY0m6aVnKPOXxyP5wiIoMU0.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Connect</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Log in with the bol account you want to connect to this sales channel and click log in.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/odPABWh4gyz8fTOUGl6PpNIkJLM.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select <strong>Offline access</strong> and click <strong>Allow</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/5Vvi0BJcciyoX4GLn7IObyZeF6c.png">
<p>All information is imported and the stock is updated to the stock recorded in ShopLinkr, provided the product already exists.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to update your stock.</strong></p>
