<p>Follow the steps below to connect your Shopify webshop to ShopLinkr. To load all products correctly, every product in Shopify needs an EAN code in the 'Barcode' field.</p>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/shopify-koppelen/6P0b37IIumCBmYAilXnQus4QoI.png">
<h3>Steps</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Sales channels" and click "Connect sales channel".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/bol-com-koppelen/KZ9zRXIeNiTvaZzNL1FWdLQoOAs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "Shopify" as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/shopify-koppelen/ooKTUwE0mWD9UKblF9SdIzCMBQ.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter your Shopify url. This is the subdomain you receive from Shopify. You can find it in your browser's address bar when you are logged in to Shopify.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/shopify-koppelen/L5RGOcnmWU3gdT3E11FfS05XBO8.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your Shopify account (if you are not logged in yet). You will see the screen below to install the ShopLinkr app. Click install.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/shopify-koppelen/8fS4r6EAXtNyje4OG3DBzSPcm2g.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>You are sent back to the page below. Select the location where ShopLinkr will apply the stock changes. This is also the location from which orders are loaded into ShopLinkr.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/shopify-koppelen/QWaaEbDtVwPFhvV9s0ouUUJ0mcc.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>All information is imported and the stock is updated to the stock in ShopLinkr, provided the product already exists.</p>
<p><strong>From this moment on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to adjust your stock.</strong></p>
