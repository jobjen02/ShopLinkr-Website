<p>Follow the steps below to connect ParcelPro to ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your ParcelPro account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the menu on the left, click "Settings" &gt; "Connections".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/parcelpro-koppelen/ZTs005foquahD4XRFkiscHobIw.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Next to the API connection, click "View".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/parcelpro-koppelen/NjugtdIwFOAVpras7TfJWgC0Q.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>You will now see your "LoginID" and "API Key". Keep these handy.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/parcelpro-koppelen/2VlIjdadu2NcKyijBhUdlST8I.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/83Tm3wTzJH2LjybQr7IFMclfY.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "ParcelPro" as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/parcelpro-koppelen/a9tBPNsi0fRlasrLaB2Lt5FCQ.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter the "User id" (LoginID) and "API Key" you received in step 4.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders will be retrieved again. This may take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
