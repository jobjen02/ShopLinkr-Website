<p>Follow the steps below to connect MyParcel with ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your MyParcel account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Shop settings" at the bottom left, then click "General".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/myparcel-koppelen/0CIRjwR0erGdUu73x1LZ3pzZLA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Copy the API key.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/myparcel-koppelen/cVUqWvwhuclm8BKuul8Ve1L9ZFc.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/dpd-koppelen/AxNcbUfoHjU8CSZDUBqKBUdKA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "MyParcel".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the "Public key" you copied in step 3. Make sure you do not include any spaces or line breaks.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are retrieved again. This may take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this moment on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
