<p>Follow the steps below to connect Sendy with ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your Sendy account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the top left, click "My Sendy".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendy-koppelen/aa4v40NfIBZnCqUI55O5GYpiI.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Personal access tokens".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendy-koppelen/4GoyIrZrmFtaO2ZCIOqxjg2eA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Add token".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendy-koppelen/hxplAcCWmxqKwup6IOLVgKWZ8.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter a name, for example "ShopLinkr", and click "Save".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendy-koppelen/cYwOMnrM90yBaunsQpwZDxE1pFQ.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>You will now receive your "Personal access token". Keep it close at hand.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/dpd-koppelen/AxNcbUfoHjU8CSZDUBqKBUdKA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "Sendy" as the type.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the "Personal access token" you received in step 6.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select your "shop".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are fetched again. This may take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
