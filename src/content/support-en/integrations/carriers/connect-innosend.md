<p>Follow the steps below to connect Innosend with ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your Innosend account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the top right, click your name and then "Settings".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/1NyVLrSk7WnfP8435YwzTHyKPs.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>On the left, click "Shops".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/CZMQODK0d6yBH7WC0JtqZ9AzSY.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "Integration".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/fSSl5NqU2gFzEFYUhsRwsFbQ.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/lfIEPCndKpMvuksqrVO75l5WM.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>You will now receive your "API Key" and "API Secret". Keep these on hand.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/7PRzhWH6AUQv7FUgmibuQRPdWbQ.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/83Tm3wTzJH2LjybQr7IFMclfY.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "Innosend" as the type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/innosend-koppelen/Lb7QyaXGKhizKKwCGmNZoTd9U1M.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter the "API Key" and "API Secret" you received in step 6.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are retrieved again. This can take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
