<ol>
<li>
<p>Log in to your Sendcloud account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the gear icon in the top right to open <strong>Settings</strong>, then click <strong>Integrations</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendcloud-koppelen/6vuCRaaIHEOxvSWySymwxrA8czM.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Find the <strong>Sendcloud API</strong> integration and click <strong>Connect</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendcloud-koppelen/Q6nPoRBvMTEy0P5HcZ738DqY.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Enter "ShopLinkr" as the name and click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/sendcloud-koppelen/kp8mLGym4DCkDAFuemp8qUtz2Gk.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>You will now receive two keys: a <strong>public key</strong> and a <strong>secret key</strong>. Keep these on hand.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Next, in ShopLinkr go to <strong>Settings &gt; Carriers</strong> and click <strong>Connect carrier</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/dpd-koppelen/AxNcbUfoHjU8CSZDUBqKBUdKA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select <strong>Sendcloud</strong> as the type.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the <strong>public key</strong> and the <strong>secret key</strong>. Make sure you do not include any spaces or line breaks.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Connect</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are refreshed from Sendcloud.</p>
