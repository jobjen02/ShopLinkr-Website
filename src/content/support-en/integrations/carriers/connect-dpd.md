<p><strong>Please note:</strong> this integration only works if you have your own contract with DPD. Don't have one yet? Get in touch with their sales department.</p>
<p>For this integration you need Shipper WS credentials from the CustomerIT department at DPD. You can reach them at <a href="mailto:customerit@dpd.nl">customerit@dpd.nl</a>. They will send you a username and password.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/dpd-koppelen/AxNcbUfoHjU8CSZDUBqKBUdKA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "DPD" as the type.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the "username" and "password" you received from DPD.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your address details. Shipping labels are created from this address.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are fetched again. This can take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
