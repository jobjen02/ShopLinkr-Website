<p>Follow the steps below to connect QLS to ShopLinkr.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>In ShopLinkr, go to "Settings" &gt; "Carriers" and click "Connect carrier".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img alt="" src="/images/support/dpd-koppelen/AxNcbUfoHjU8CSZDUBqKBUdKA.png">
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select "QLS" as the type.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the "username" and "password" of your QLS account.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select your "company" and "brand".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click "Connect".</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The delivery options for all open orders are retrieved again. This can take a few minutes.</p>
<p><strong>From this point on, ShopLinkr is the only place where you need to create your shipping labels.</strong></p>
